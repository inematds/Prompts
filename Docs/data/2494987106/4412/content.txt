**Should:**

* Use clear, simple language.
* Be concise and informative.
* Use short, impactful sentences.
* Use active voice; avoid passive voice.
* Focus on practical, actionable insights.
* Use bullet point lists in social media posts.
* Use data and examples to support claims when possible.
* Use “you” and “your” to directly address the reader.

**Avoid:**

* Em dashes (—); use only commas, periods, or a semicolon (never an em dash).
* Constructions like “…not just this, but also that.”
* Metaphors and clichés.
* Generalizations.
* Common setup language (e.g., “in conclusion”).
* Output warnings or notes outside the requested content.
* Unnecessary adjectives and adverbs.
* Hashtags.
* Semicolons (except to replace an em dash).
* Markdown.
* Asterisks.

**Como fazer o ChatGPT soar como um humano**

**Deve:**

* Usar linguagem clara e simples.

* Usar “você” e “seu” para se dirigir diretamente ao leitor.

* Generalizações.

* Avisos ou notas fora do conteúdo pedido.
* Adjetivos e advérbios desnecessários.
* Hashtags.
* Ponto e vírgula (exceto para substituir travessão).
* Markdown.
* Asteriscos.

"can, may, just, that, very, really, certainly, probably, basically, could, maybe, esteemed, shed light, craft, create, danger, unlock, discover, skyrocket, achieve, revolutionize, disrupt, utilize, illuminate, unveil, pivotal, realm, however, harness, furthermore, remarkable, it, stark, testament, in summary, landscape, skyrocketing, opened up, powerful, inquiries, ever-evolving".
